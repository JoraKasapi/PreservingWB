PreservingWesternBalkans - Team


The project/ website PreservingWB.city, including in its structure preservingtirana.city and preservingpristina.city, was supported through the second RYCO Open Call for Project Proposals also Implemented and organized by Open Labs Albania in Tirana and IPKO Foundation in Pristina.

This represents an open and public project therefore its original initiators will be mentioned, but the project will still be ongoing and enriched with its future contributors.
